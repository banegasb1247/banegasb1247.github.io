About the Movie

Quick Summary

The Dark Knight follows Batman as he faces the Joker, a criminal mastermind who pushes Gotham into chaos. With help from allies like Commissioner Gordon and Harvey Dent, Batman tries to stop the Joker while struggling with the cost of being a hero.

Why I Picked This Movie

  1. It has strong characters and intense stakes.
  2. The Joker is one of the most memorable villains.
  3. The story shows tough choices and consequences.
